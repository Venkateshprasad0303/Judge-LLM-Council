Council of LLMs — System Architecture
7-Phase adversarial multi-agent legal reasoning pipeline
Pipeline
1
Intelligence Agent
Classifies the query, identifies legal issues, parties, jurisdiction, and decides how many web searches to run. Accepts conversation history for follow-up queries.
Sonnet 4.6 Extended thinking (3k–12k)
Output → IntelBundle: caseType · legalIssues[] · burdenOfProof · complexityLevel · tavilyConfig
2
Research Fan-out
Three researchers run in parallel against the RAG knowledge base (51 US law docs) and Tavily web search. A synthesizer merges all findings. Source IDs are normalized to src-N for citation tracing.
Haiku 4.5 ×3 (parallel) Sonnet 4.6 (synthesis) Tavily 0–9 searches RAG (51 docs)
Output → ResearchBundle: sources[src-1…N] · keyStatutes[] · precedents[] · plaintiff/defenseStrengths[]
3  ·  Advocate Chains
3 parallel · each: Build → Critique → Refine
Plaintiff
o4-mini (build)

Haiku (critique)

o4-mini (refine)
Defense
Gemini 2.5 Pro (build)

Haiku (critique)

Gemini 2.5 Pro (refine)
Neutral
Sonnet 4.6 (build)

Haiku (critique)

Sonnet 4.6 (refine)
Output → AdvocateBundle: {plaintiff · defense · neutral} × {argument · keyPoints[] · citations[]}
4
Cross-Examination + Consensus Measurement
Each advocate attacks the other two. Then a consensus step reads all positions and rebuttals to measure how much advocates agreed and which specific claims were contested.
Haiku 4.5 ×3 (rebuttals) Sonnet 4.6 (consensus)
Output → CrossExam: rebuttals[] · consensusLevel · divergentClaims[] · agreedFacts[]
5  ·  Judge Panel
3 parallel specialists · each given one narrow question
Fact Judge
Sonnet 4.6

+ Extended thinking
Disputed vs. undisputed facts only
Law Judge
o4-mini
Applicable statutes, standards, legal tests
Precedent Judge
Gemini 2.5 Pro
Analogous cases and which side they favor
Output → JudgePanel: factJudge · lawJudge · precedentJudge · minorityFinding flag
6
Chief Judge  — reads all prior phases
Synthesizes everything into a formal ruling. Streams output token by token. Escalates to Opus when the case is complex and consensus is low (~20% of queries).
Sonnet 4.6 + thinking (default, ~80%) Opus 4.6 + 16k thinking (complex + high disagreement, ~20%)
Output → Ruling: findingsOfFact[] · conclusionsOfLaw[] · ruling · citedAuthorities[src-N] · epistemicMarkers[] · minorityReport
7  ·  Post-Ruling Checks
2 parallel · runs after Chief Judge
7a   Devil's Advocate Critique
Haiku reads the ruling with a contrarian prompt. Surfaces assumptions, missed arguments, biases, and overconfident claims. Advisory only — does not change the ruling.
Haiku 4.5
7b   Citation Integrity Check
Every citedAuthority is verified against the ResearchBundle src-N IDs. Programmatic check. If unverified citations found, Haiku is re-prompted once to replace them.
ProgrammaticHaiku re-prompt (if needed)
